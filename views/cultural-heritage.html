Cultural Heritage

The UCR Library is interested in identifying, preserving, interpreting, and sharing the rich cultural legacies of diverse communities in Riverside and San Bernardino Counties, a geographical region also known as Inland Southern California. We have significant collections in local inland history, history of the local citrus and other agricultural industries, and the Rupert Costo Library of the American Indian. We are also interested in preserving underrepresented communities and cultures.
